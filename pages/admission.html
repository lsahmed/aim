Welcome to Ardy University of Munich

We are delighted that you are considering joining our university. Our admissions process is designed to help us identify students who are a good fit for our programs and our community.

How to Apply

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit your academic transcripts and test scores.
  3. Provide a personal statement and letters of recommendation.
  4. Pay the application fee.

School Application

  1. Complete the online or offline application.
  2. Submit your academic transcripts and test scores.
  3. crack the entrance exam of AET
  4. Provide a personal statement.
  5. Pay the application fee.

Important Dates

  • Fall Semester Application Deadline: March 15
  • Spring Semester Application Deadline: October 15

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the admissions process, please contact us at [admissions@ardy.edu](mailto:admissions@ardy.edu).
