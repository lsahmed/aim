Admission

To apply for admission to Ardy Institute of Munich, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website and click on the "Admission" tab.
  2. Create an account and complete the online application form.
  3. Upload all required documents, including transcripts, test scores, and a statement of purpose.
  4. Pay the application fee.
  5. Submit your application and wait for a decision.

For more information, please contact our admissions office at admissions@ardy-institute.com.

Result

To check your result, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website and click on the "Result" tab.
  2. Enter your admit no.
  3. Click on the "Submit" button.
  4. View your result and print a copy for your records.

If you have any questions about your result, please contact our registrar's office at registrar@ardy-institute.com.

Eligibility

Candidates must have a high school diploma or equivalent with a minimum GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) to be eligible for admission to our undergraduate programs.

Candidates must have a bachelor's degree in a relevant field with a minimum GPA of 3.2 (out of 4.0) to be eligible for admission to our graduate programs.

The syllabus for each program varies depending on the field of study. Please refer to our website for program-specific syllabus information.
